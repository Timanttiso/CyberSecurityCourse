# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: 8.3.2026_

This Cookie Policy explains how **Reservation Company** (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you access or use our reservation web application (“Service”).

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They help the Service function properly, improve security, and enhance your user experience.

Cookies may be:

- **Session cookies** — deleted when you close your browser  
- **Persistent cookies** — remain on your device for a set period  
- **First‑party cookies** — set by us  

---

## 2. Cookies We Use

We aim to use only essential cookies necessary for the operation and security of the Service.

### 2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the Service to function and cannot be disabled:

- **Authentication cookies**  
  Used to keep you logged in and maintain your session.

- **Security cookies**  
  Used to protect your account and prevent fraudulent activity.

- **Preference cookies**  
  Used to remember basic settings such as your selected role or interface preferences.

## 3. Legal Basis for Using Cookies

Under GDPR, we may store essential cookies on your device because they are necessary for the operation of the Service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

For any non‑essential cookies, we will request your consent before storing them (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- Block cookies  
- Delete existing cookies  
- Receive notifications before cookies are stored  

Disabling essential cookies may cause parts of the Service to stop working properly.
